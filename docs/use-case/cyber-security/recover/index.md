Recovery is where business continuity commitments are proven: how
quickly and safely critical services can return to normal.

## Outcomes

- Faster restoration of critical services with validated integrity
- Reduced data loss and improved confidence in recovery processes
- Improved disaster recovery (DR) and business continuity (BCP)
  readiness
- Measurable resilience improvements after incidents

## What this includes

- Service restoration and validation
- Backup restore and integrity checks
- Disaster recovery and business continuity planning
- Resilience improvements and hardening based on incidents
