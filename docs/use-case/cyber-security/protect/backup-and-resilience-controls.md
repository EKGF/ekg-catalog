Backups are only valuable if they can be restored quickly and safely.
This use case focuses on resilience controls that reduce downtime and
limit data loss when incidents occur.

## Outcomes

- Faster restoration of critical services after disruption
- Reduced data loss aligned to recovery objectives (RTO/RPO)
- Improved readiness against ransomware and destructive attacks
- Higher confidence through regular recovery testing

## Typical scope

- Recovery objectives for critical services (tiers and expectations)
- Backup coverage, retention, and segregation practices
- Restore testing and validation (including integrity checks)
- Operational ownership and runbooks for recovery
