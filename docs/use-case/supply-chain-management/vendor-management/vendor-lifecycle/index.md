## The Challenge

Organizations face significant challenges in vendor lifecycle
management:

- **Lifecycle tracking** — Tracking vendors through different
  lifecycle stages (onboarding, active, suspended, offboarding)
- **Status management** — Managing vendor status and lifecycle
  transitions
- **Offboarding complexity** — Offboarding involves multiple steps
  including contract termination, system access removal, and data
  archival
- **Data consistency** — Ensuring vendor data consistency across
  lifecycle stages
- **Compliance** — Ensuring compliance requirements are met at each
  lifecycle stage
- **Historical tracking** — Maintaining historical records of vendor
  lifecycle changes
- **Data fragmentation** — Vendor lifecycle information scattered
  across multiple systems

Traditional vendor lifecycle management systems provide limited
lifecycle tracking and lack the integrated view needed for
comprehensive lifecycle management.

## Why EKG is Required

Enterprise Knowledge Graphs provide powerful vendor lifecycle
capabilities:

- **Unified lifecycle view** — Connect all vendor lifecycle
  information across systems and time periods
- **Lifecycle modeling** — Model vendor lifecycle stages and
  transitions through graph relationships
- **Status tracking** — Track vendor status and lifecycle transitions
  through graph relationships
- **Historical records** — Maintain historical records of vendor
  lifecycle changes using graph temporal relationships
- **Workflow automation** — Use graph relationships to automate
  lifecycle workflows
- **Compliance tracking** — Track compliance requirements at each
  lifecycle stage
- **Data consistency** — Ensure vendor data consistency across
  lifecycle stages through semantic relationships

## Business Value

- **Lifecycle visibility** — Clear visibility into vendor lifecycle
  stages and transitions
- **Operational efficiency** — Automated lifecycle management
  processes reduce manual effort
- **Compliance assurance** — Better lifecycle tracking ensures
  compliance at each stage
- **Data quality** — Consistent vendor data across lifecycle stages
  improves data quality
- **Risk management** — Understanding vendor lifecycle enables risk
  management
