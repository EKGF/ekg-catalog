## The Challenge

Organizations face challenges in financial fundamentals management:

- **Data volume** — Entities generate large volumes of financial data
  across multiple periods
- **Data quality** — Ensuring accuracy and consistency of financial
  data across systems
- **Multiple standards** — Different accounting standards (GAAP, IFRS)
  and reporting requirements
- **Temporal tracking** — Maintaining historical financial data and
  tracking changes over time
- **Ratio calculation** — Calculating and maintaining financial ratios
  and metrics
- **Data integration** — Integrating financial data from multiple
  sources and systems
- **Relationship context** — Understanding how entity relationships
  affect financial fundamentals
- **Regulatory requirements** — Meeting regulatory requirements for
  financial reporting and disclosure

## Why EKG is Required

Enterprise Knowledge Graphs provide powerful financial fundamentals
management capabilities:

- **Unified financial view** — Connect all financial data across
  systems, periods, and entities
- **Temporal tracking** — Track financial data over time and maintain
  historical records
- **Ratio calculation** — Automatically calculate financial ratios and
  metrics from connected data
- **Relationship analysis** — Understand how entity relationships
  affect financial fundamentals
- **Multi-entity comparison** — Compare financial fundamentals across
  entities and time periods
- **Standard integration** — Link financial data to accounting
  standards and reporting requirements
- **Data quality** — Identify and resolve financial data
  inconsistencies through relationship analysis
- **Regulatory integration** — Connect financial fundamentals to
  regulatory requirements and filings
