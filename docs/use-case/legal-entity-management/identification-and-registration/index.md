## The Challenge

Organizations face significant challenges in managing entity
identification and registration:

- **Multiple jurisdictions** — Entities may be registered in multiple
  jurisdictions with different requirements
- **Registration complexity** — Different types of registrations
  (incorporation, business license, tax registration) across
  jurisdictions
- **Data fragmentation** — Registration information scattered across
  multiple systems and jurisdictions
- **Regulatory requirements** — Different jurisdictions have different
  registration and disclosure requirements
- **Data quality** — Maintaining accurate and up-to-date registration
  information across systems
- **Temporal tracking** — Tracking registration status and changes
  over time
- **Classification systems** — Managing entity classifications across
  multiple taxonomies and standards

## Why EKG is Required

Enterprise Knowledge Graphs provide powerful capabilities for
identification and registration management:

- **Unified registration view** — Connect all registration information
  across jurisdictions and systems
- **Temporal tracking** — Track registration status and changes over
  time
- **Multi-jurisdictional integration** — Link registrations across
  multiple jurisdictions
- **Classification management** — Model and manage entity
  classifications across multiple taxonomies
- **Regulatory integration** — Link registrations to regulatory
  requirements and filing obligations
- **Data quality** — Identify and resolve registration data
  inconsistencies
- **Entity verification** — Support entity verification through
  comprehensive registration data

## Business Value

- **Regulatory compliance** — Meet requirements for entity
  identification and registration
- **Risk assessment** — Support risk assessment through accurate
  entity identification
- **Due diligence** — Enable comprehensive due diligence through
  registration information
- **Operational efficiency** — Automate registration tracking and
  reduce manual management
- **Data quality** — Improve registration data quality through
  validation and deduplication
- **Legal compliance** — Maintain legal entity status through proper
  registration management
