## The Challenge

Organizations face challenges in M&A history management:

- **Transaction complexity** — M&A transactions involve multiple
  parties, structures, and regulatory approvals
- **Temporal tracking** — Tracking M&A transactions, announcements,
  approvals, and completions over time
- **Transaction types** — Different types of transactions (mergers,
  acquisitions, divestitures, joint ventures)
- **Regulatory requirements** — M&A transactions require regulatory
  approvals and filings across jurisdictions
- **Relationship impact** — Understanding how M&A transactions affect
  entity relationships and structures
- **Data fragmentation** — M&A information scattered across legal,
  financial, and regulatory systems
- **Historical reconstruction** — Reconstructing entity history
  through M&A transactions
- **Data quality** — Maintaining accurate and complete M&A transaction
  records

## Why EKG is Required

Enterprise Knowledge Graphs provide powerful M&A history management
capabilities:

- **Transaction modeling** — Model complex M&A transactions,
  structures, and parties
- **Temporal tracking** — Track M&A transactions over time and
  maintain historical records
- **Party linking** — Link M&A transactions to entities, acquirers,
  targets, and related parties
- **Regulatory integration** — Link M&A transactions to regulatory
  approvals and filings
- **Relationship analysis** — Understand how M&A transactions affect
  entity relationships and structures
- **Historical reconstruction** — Reconstruct entity history through
  M&A transaction chains
- **Document linking** — Connect M&A documents and filings to
  transaction records
- **Multi-entity analysis** — Analyze M&A patterns and trends across
  entity portfolios

## Business Value

- **Due diligence** — Support due diligence through comprehensive M&A
  history
- **Risk assessment** — Assess risk based on M&A history and
  transaction patterns
- **Regulatory compliance** — Meet requirements for M&A reporting and
  disclosure
- **Entity analysis** — Understand entity evolution and structure
  through M&A history
- **Strategic insights** — Understand M&A patterns and trends across
  entities
- **Historical reconstruction** — Reconstruct complete entity history
  for analysis and reporting
