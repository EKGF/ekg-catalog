## The Challenge

Organizations face challenges in stock split management:

- **Split types** — Different types of stock splits (forward splits,
  reverse splits) with different ratios
- **Temporal tracking** — Tracking stock split announcements,
  ex-dividend dates, and effective dates
- **Ownership adjustment** — Adjusting historical ownership records
  for stock splits
- **Price adjustment** — Adjusting historical share prices for stock
  splits
- **Regulatory requirements** — Different jurisdictions have different
  requirements for stock split reporting
- **Relationship context** — Understanding how stock splits affect
  ownership and share structures
- **Historical reconstruction** — Reconstructing historical ownership
  and price data adjusted for splits
- **Data quality** — Maintaining accurate and complete stock split
  records
