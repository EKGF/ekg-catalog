## The Challenge

Organizations face significant challenges in contact and location
information management:

- **Multiple locations** — Entities may have multiple addresses
  (registered, business, mailing, operational)
- **Contact diversity** — Entities have multiple types of contacts
  with different roles and responsibilities
- **Data quality** — Maintaining accurate and up-to-date contact and
  location information
- **Address changes** — Addresses and contacts change over time
  through relocations and personnel changes
- **Geographic context** — Understanding addresses in context of
  jurisdictions, regions, and countries
- **Regulatory requirements** — Different jurisdictions require
  different contact and location information
- **Privacy regulations** — Managing contact data in compliance with
  GDPR and other privacy regulations

## Why EKG is Required

Enterprise Knowledge Graphs provide powerful contact and location
information management capabilities:

- **Unified contact view** — Connect all contact and location
  information across systems
- **Address relationships** — Link addresses to identify shared
  locations and entity networks
- **Geographic integration** — Connect addresses to jurisdictions,
  regions, and countries
- **Temporal tracking** — Track contact and location changes over time
- **Contact relationships** — Link contacts to entities, roles, and
  other contacts
- **Regulatory integration** — Link contact and location information
  to regulatory requirements
- **Data quality** — Identify and resolve contact and location data
  inconsistencies

## Business Value

- **Regulatory compliance** — Meet requirements for contact and
  location information collection and maintenance
- **Communication efficiency** — Ensure accurate contact information
  for effective communication
- **Geographic insights** — Understand entity geographic presence and
  distribution
- **Risk assessment** — Assess geographic risk based on entity
  locations
- **Data quality** — Improve contact and location data quality through
  validation and deduplication
- **Operational efficiency** — Automate contact and location
  management and reduce manual data entry
