# Contacts

## Summary

Contact management for legal entities involves maintaining information about individuals associated with entities, including directors, officers, shareholders, authorized signatories, and other key personnel. Contact information is essential for communication, regulatory compliance, corporate governance, and understanding entity relationships.

## The Challenge

Organizations face challenges in legal entity contact management:

- **Contact diversity** — Entities have multiple types of contacts with different roles and responsibilities
- **Role changes** — Contact roles change over time (appointments, resignations, terminations)
- **Multiple entities** — Individuals may be contacts for multiple entities
- **Data quality** — Maintaining accurate and up-to-date contact information
- **Privacy regulations** — Managing contact data in compliance with GDPR and other privacy regulations
- **Relationship complexity** — Understanding relationships between contacts, entities, and other individuals
- **Communication management** — Ensuring appropriate communication channels and preferences
- **Regulatory requirements** — Different jurisdictions require different contact information for filings

## Why EKG is Required

Enterprise Knowledge Graphs provide powerful contact management capabilities:

- **Relationship modeling** — Connect contacts to entities, roles, and other contacts
- **Role tracking** — Track contact roles and responsibilities over time
- **Multi-entity view** — Understand contacts across entity networks and relationships
- **Temporal tracking** — Maintain historical records of contact appointments and changes
- **Privacy management** — Link contacts to privacy preferences and consent records
- **Communication integration** — Connect contacts to communication history and preferences
- **Regulatory integration** — Link contacts to regulatory requirements and filing obligations
- **Network analysis** — Identify contact networks and relationships across entities

## Business Value

- **Regulatory compliance** — Meet requirements for contact information collection and maintenance
- **Communication efficiency** — Ensure accurate contact information for effective communication
- **Corporate governance** — Support corporate governance through accurate contact management
- **Relationship insights** — Understand contact relationships and networks across entities
- **Data quality** — Improve contact data quality through validation and deduplication
- **Operational efficiency** — Automate contact management and reduce manual data entry
