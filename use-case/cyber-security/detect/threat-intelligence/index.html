    Threat Intelligence

    Use threat intelligence to anticipate relevant threats, prioritize defenses, and enrich investigations, focusing on what matters to the organization’s industry, footprint, and critical services.

    Threat intelligence creates value when it is relevant and actionable: it helps the business decide where to focus defenses and how urgently to act.

    Outcomes¶

    • Faster awareness of emerging threats relevant to the organization
    • Better prioritization of remediation and detection efforts
    • Improved investigations through enrichment and context
    • Reduced wasted effort on low-relevance intelligence noise

    Typical scope¶

    • Intelligence sources and relevance criteria
    • Enrichment of alerts and cases with intel context
    • Prioritization of actions (patching, blocking, monitoring changes)
    • Reporting for business stakeholders (what changed, what it means)

    GenAI-enabled execution¶

    Agents can summarize intel, assess relevance against the organization’s profile, and draft recommended actions—guardrailed by explicit acceptance criteria and human review for disruptive changes.
